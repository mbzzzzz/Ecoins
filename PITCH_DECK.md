## üí° The Solution: Ecoins

Ecoins is a dual-sided marketplace that uses **Computer Vision AI** to verify real-world eco-actions instantly.

### How It Works (The Core Loop)
1.  **Action**: A user performs a green task (e.g., uses a reusable cup, cycles to work).
2.  **Verification**: The user snaps a photo. Our proprietary **AI Auditing System** (powered by Llama 3.2 & Gemini Vision) analyzes the image, cross-references GPS/Time metadata, and validates the claim with >85% confidence.
3.  **Reward**: The user earns **"Ecoins"** and a verifiable "Carbon Saved" certificate on their profile.
4.  **Redemption**: Users spend Ecoins on exclusive discounts from partner brands who want to target verified eco-consumers.
